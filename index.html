Unforgettable Experiences and Breathtaking Moments

Japan is a truly timeless country, a place where ancient traditions are blended with modern life. More than three quarters of the 126m population live in sprawling cities (including the capital Tokyo and the industrial centre of Osaka) on the coastal fringes of Japan's four mountainous, heavily-wooded islands. Having been culturally influenced by its neighbors for a long period, today the country blends its ancient traditions with various aspects of Western life.
